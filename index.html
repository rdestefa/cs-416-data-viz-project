Gaming Hours Over the Years

I would consider myself a pretty avid gamer, but over the past several years my gaming habits have changed rather interestingly. Whether my interests shifted from genre to genre, or certain life events impacting my gaming frequency, I have not been consistent in how I use my free time to game. Over the past 7 years, I've been recording the number of hours I spend on each video game and I've organized them by genre and platform.
